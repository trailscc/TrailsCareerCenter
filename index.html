Our Mission

TRAILS Career Center is dedicated to creating transformative educational pathways that empower youth and young adults to build professional careers in cutting-edge technology. Through comprehensive training in AI, robotics, coding, and entrepreneurship, we're building a bridge from community challenges to skilled, contributing professionals.
